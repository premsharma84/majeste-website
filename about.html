About APS Majeste

An Indian premium personal care brand

APS Majeste is committed to creating high-quality fragrances and skincare products that combine luxury, performance, and affordability. Founded with the vision of making premium self-care accessible to everyone.

Our Philosophy

Luxury Within Everyone's Reach

At APS Majeste, we believe luxury is not defined by price—it's defined by quality, confidence, and the way it makes you feel. Our philosophy is built on making premium fragrances and skincare accessible to every Indian, without compromising on craftsmanship or performance.

Every APS Majeste creation is thoughtfully formulated with carefully selected ingredients and modern science to deliver long-lasting fragrance, effective skincare, and an elevated self-care experience. We blend sophistication with everyday practicality, creating products that complement every lifestyle and every personality.

Because true luxury isn't reserved for a few—it's something everyone deserves to experience. With APS Majeste, every spray, every cleanse, and every moment is an expression of confidence, elegance, and individuality.

Our Brand Story

Building the Future of Premium Beauty in India

APS Majeste was founded with a simple vision—to make premium personal care accessible to every Indian.

We believe luxury should not be defined by price, but by quality, experience, and confidence. While many premium beauty products remain out of reach for millions of consumers, we saw an opportunity to create a brand that delivers sophisticated fragrances, effective skincare, elegant packaging, and exceptional value at an accessible price.

Every APS Majeste product is thoughtfully crafted using carefully selected ingredients and modern formulations to help people look their best, feel confident, and express their unique personality. Whether it's a signature fragrance or an everyday skincare essential, our products are designed to elevate daily self-care.

Our long-term vision extends beyond manufacturing premium fragrances and skincare products. APS Majeste aims to build one of India's leading beauty and personal care retail ecosystems through a strong distributor network, branded retail stores, modern trade, and digital commerce. Every distributor who joins us today becomes a partner in this long-term growth journey.

As our network grows across India, we are committed to creating lasting value for customers, empowering our distributor and retail partners, and building a scalable business with sustainable growth.
