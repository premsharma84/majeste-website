Our Philosophy

A quiet rebellion against fast beauty

We believe skincare should be slow, intentional, and made well. Every APS MAJESTE formula begins in the fields of Udaipur, where growers we know by name harvest botanicals at the precise moment of their potency. We then blend in small batches — never more than 200 units — so every bottle is fresh, traceable, and made by a person who cared.

Our chemists pair these botanicals with proven actives like bakuchiol, niacinamide, and triple-weight hyaluronic acid. The result is skincare that feels like a ritual and performs like a treatment — without compromise.

Frequently asked

What you'd like to know

Clear answers to the questions our community asks most. Can't find what you need? Contact our concierge.

Are APS MAJESTE products suitable for sensitive skin?
Yes. Every formula is patch-tested on reactive and sensitive skin panels and formulated without common irritants — no essential oils at high concentrations, no synthetic fragrance, no drying alcohols. The Hydra Veil Moisturizer and Neroli Radiance Face Wash are particularly well-tolerated by reactive skin.
Is APS MAJESTE vegan and cruelty-free?
All APS MAJESTE products are cruelty-free and certified by Leaping Bunny, and every formula in the collection is vegan — we use plant-derived alternatives to beeswax and other animal by-products across all serums, face wash, moisturizers, perfumes, and body oils.
Where are APS MAJESTE products made?
All formulas are blended, filled, and finished in our atelier in Udaipur, India, in batches of no more than 200 units. Fragrance bottling and quality control are completed in our Udaipur atelier before shipping. Each bottle is numbered and signed by its maker.
Where can I buy APS MAJESTE products?
All APS MAJESTE products are available on Amazon India. Click the "Buy on Amazon" button on any product page to view current pricing, offers, and delivery options. Amazon Prime members get free fast delivery.
Do you offer returns or exchanges?
Returns and exchanges are handled directly through Amazon India's return policy, which typically allows returns within 7–10 days of delivery. Visit your Amazon Orders page to initiate a return. For product-specific questions or concerns, our concierge is happy to help at concierge@apsmajeste.in.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery is fulfilled by Amazon India. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days across India, with faster options (1–2 day delivery) available for Prime members in major cities. Delivery times to remote areas may be longer.
Are your products made in India?
Yes — every APS MAJESTE product is formulated and manufactured in our Udaipur atelier in Rajasthan. Our ingredients are sourced from across India: saffron from Kashmir, sandalwood from Mysore, jasmine from Tamil Nadu, and rose from Kannauj.
