Quick answers

Before you write

What are your response hours?
Our concierge team replies Monday through Saturday, between 10am and 7pm Eastern Time. Messages received outside these hours are answered the next business day, typically within 24 hours.
Can I book a fragrance consultation?
Yes. We offer complimentary 30-minute consultations by phone or video with one of our fragrance specialists. Mention "fragrance consultation" in the subject line and we will share three available time slots.
Do you offer wholesale and retail partnerships?
We partner with a small number of curated retailers each year. Write to partnerships@apsmajeste.example.com with your store details, brand list, and why APS MAJESTE fits your customers. We review applications monthly.
Can I visit the New York atelier?
Yes — our Mercer Street atelier is open Monday through Saturday, 10am to 7pm. We offer complimentary 20-minute consultations and fragrance discovery sessions. Walk-ins are welcome; appointments can be reserved by phone or email.
