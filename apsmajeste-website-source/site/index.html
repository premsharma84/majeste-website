Our Philosophy

A quiet rebellion against fast beauty

We believe skincare should be slow, intentional, and made well. Every APS MAJESTE formula begins in the fields of Provence, where growers we know by name harvest botanicals at the precise moment of their potency. We then blend in small batches — never more than 200 units — so every bottle is fresh, traceable, and made by a person who cared.

Our chemists pair these botanicals with proven actives like bakuchiol, niacinamide, and triple-weight hyaluronic acid. The result is skincare that feels like a ritual and performs like a treatment — without compromise.

Frequently asked

What you'd like to know

Clear answers to the questions our community asks most. Can't find what you need? Contact our concierge.

Are APS MAJESTE products suitable for sensitive skin?
Yes. Every formula is patch-tested on reactive and sensitive skin panels and formulated without common irritants — no essential oils at high concentrations, no synthetic fragrance, no drying alcohols. The Hydra Veil Moisturizer and Neroli Radiance Face Wash are particularly well-tolerated by reactive skin.
Is APS MAJESTE vegan and cruelty-free?
All APS MAJESTE products are cruelty-free and certified by Leaping Bunny, and every formula in the collection is vegan — we use plant-derived alternatives to beeswax and other animal by-products across all serums, face wash, moisturizers, perfumes, and body oils.
Where are APS MAJESTE products made?
All formulas are blended, filled, and finished in our atelier in Provence, France, in batches of no more than 200 units. Fragrance bottling and quality control are completed in our New York atelier before shipping. Each bottle is numbered and signed by its maker.
What is your return policy?
We offer 30-day returns on unopened products. If a product doesn't suit your skin, contact our concierge within 30 days of delivery and we will arrange a complimentary return or exchange. Sample sachets are included with every order so you can patch-test before opening full-size products.
How long does shipping take?
Orders ship within 48 hours from our New York atelier. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days within the United States and 7–14 business days internationally. All orders ship carbon-neutral, and shipping is complimentary on orders over $95.
Do you offer refills?
Yes. Our 50ml eau de parfum flacons, facial oils, and body oils are refillable. Refills are available at 30% off the retail price and ship in compostable pouches. Sign in to your account to manage auto-refills.
