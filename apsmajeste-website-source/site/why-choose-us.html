The Case for Slower Skincare

Why people choose APS MAJESTE, and stay

We make a handful of products the right way, then make them again the next year — only better. Here is what that means in practice, and why it matters.

Clean, by our definition

No parabens, sulfates, phthalates, mineral oil, or synthetic dyes. No undisclosed fragrance. We publish full INCI lists and the source of every ingredient on each product page — not because we have to, but because you should know what is on your skin.

Named, not anonymous

Every botanical in a APS MAJESTE formula is traceable to the farm that grew it. Our rose comes from Maria in Grasse. Our jasmine from the Bertaud family in Eze. Our neroli from a cooperative outside Nice. Names, not lot numbers.

Small batches, signed

We blend in batches of 200 — small enough that our chemists can taste, smell, and adjust each one. Every bottle carries a batch number and the signature of the person who made it. If something is ever off, we can trace it to the exact week and the exact hands.

Clinically tested, honestly reported

Every formula undergoes independent dermatological testing for tolerance, hydration, and barrier repair. We report the actual results — including the percentages — not the cherry-picked ones. If a claim isn't backed by data, we don't make it.

Refillable by design

Weighted glass flacons. Brass caps machined to last a lifetime. Refills at 30% off, shipped in compostable pouches. We design packaging to be kept, not thrown away — and to cost less the longer you stay with us.

Carbon neutral, end to end

From farm to atelier to your door, every APS MAJESTE order ships carbon-neutral. We measure, reduce, and offset the remainder with reforestation projects in the Luberon and the Atlantic Forest. We publish our annual carbon report on our about page.

How we make it

From field to flacon, in three movements

A single batch of APS MAJESTE takes between four and nine months to make. Here is what happens in that time.

Harvest at peak

Our growers harvest each botanical at the precise moment of its active potency — roses before sunrise, jasmine at dusk, neroli in the cool morning. We pay above fair-trade rates and pick within 24 hours of cutting.

Blend in small batches

In our Provence atelier, our chemists cold-press, distill, and blend in batches of no more than 200 units. Each batch is tasted, smelled, and tested before it is approved for filling.

Test, sign, ship

Every batch undergoes independent dermatological testing. Once cleared, each bottle is filled, numbered, and signed by its maker — then shipped carbon-neutral from our New York atelier.
